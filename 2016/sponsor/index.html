We would very much like to seek your help in sponsoring these expenses.

Hong Kong Creative Open Technology Association (HKCOTA) is a non- profit (pending) organisation founded in 2014 to promote Open Standards; Free and Open Source Software; Open Hardware; Free Culture Works, Open Content and Creative Commons.

Open Source Hong Kong (OSHK) is a community in aim of advocacy and promotion on concepts and uses of open source, to organise meet-ups, seminars, conferences and other events in Hong Kong and Asia. OSHK encourages communication and collaboration of open source, open technology and open culture related communities in Hong Kong and the world. OSHK established 2 annual conferences - Hong Kong Open Source Conference (HKOSCon) in 2013 and PyCon HK in 2015.
