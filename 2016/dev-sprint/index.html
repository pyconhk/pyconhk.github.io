Development Sprint

Besides 2 tracks of talks, you may bring your own laptops and do some hacking / coding on python projects in your own at Development Sprints. It is also an opportunity to meet with other developers.

Time and Location

Day 1 (10/29 Sat): 12:10 - 17:10 at Workshop Track (Training Room 1-3)
Day 2 (10/30 Sun): 13:00 - 17:30 at Track 2 (Training Theatre)

If you are interested to tell your projects to others, please fill out this google form, and it will be listed publicly on this google spreadsheet.
