Participate

To participate, you can purchase a ticket online and attend the conference. In general, we provides regular tickets, early bird tickets, patron tickets, student tickets.

Regular Ticket is HKD 300 each. (Early Birds: HKD 200)

Above ticket types of PyCon HK 2016 are available for sales on Eventbrite.

Patrons

PyCon HK is not only limited to local speakers and participants, and we also invites overseas participants to speak or attend our conference. Both of local and overseas participants can submit proposals during CFP period, and PyCon HK also invites some overseas speakers to visit and speak PyCon HK every year.

PyCon HK provides financial aids program to speakers and attendees. The financial aids program is aimed to (but not limited to) sponsor the travel costs of some overseas speakers, and ticket costs of some participants who apply for financial aids program.

Therefore, patron tickets (HKD 600) are available for sales to raise up the funding to financial aids program and PyCon HK. Patrons (individual/company name) will be listed on conference website.

Students

To encourage students to learn & use python and attend the PyCon HK, we provides student tickets (HKD 100) to full-time students.

Contact PyCon HK

To contact with PyCon HK, please feel freely to email to pycon@pycon.hk.
