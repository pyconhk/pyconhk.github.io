Call For Proposals

PyCon HK 2016 provides keynotes, talks, workshops and networking opportunities to participants.

To call for speakers, PyCon HK uses "calls for proposals" (CFP) process, inviting guest speakers, and "calls for sponsors" (CFS) to arrange the program of PyCon HK.

Submission Deadline of PyCon HK 2016 Call For Proposals extended to 14 August 2016. Proposers will receive first email before CFP deadline regarding to CFP progress. Period of open vote by Early Bird Participants is 15 August to 21 August. 2 English & 1 Cantonese talk proposals and 1 English & 1 Cantonese talk proposals received the most of votes will be selected first. And for other proposals, PyCon HK 2016 committee will consider the result of open vote by early birds and select other topics. Result of Call For Proposals will be announced at the end of August.

Result of CFP

Result of Call For Proposals will be announced and notified speakers at the end of August, 2 proposals are also selected in September from pending list.

  5. DeepWhale: Recognizing and Localizing Endangered Right Whales with Extremely Deep Neural Networks (Cantonese) (Felix Lau)

  11. (Switched to Workshop) Workshop: Massive Open Online Course Architecture using Python + Web Platform (English) (Rizky Ariestiyansyah) (Indonesia)
  12. (Switched to Workshop) Workshop: AIOrchestra. Lesson learn while using AsyncIO coroutines on dynamic sequenced tasks (English) (Denys Makogon) (Ukraine)

Proposal #1 to #4 are picked from Early Bird vetting scheme.

26 proposals are received through CFP process. Early birds before 1 August can cast their votes, and proposals with most of votes are selected for English and Chinese tracks. And then conference team will start its vetting process.
